How It Works

At SikshaHub, our platform makes finding the right course simple and personalized. Follow these steps to discover how we can help you unlock your true potential:

Step 1: Take the Assessement

Start by taking our comprehensive smart quiz, designed to assess your strengths, weaknesses, and interests. The quiz covers various subjects to understand your preferences and areas for improvement.

Step 2: Discover Your Profile

Once you complete the quiz, we'll provide you with a detailed profile highlighting your strengths, interests, and areas that need focus. This profile helps you understand your learning style and preferences better.

Step 3: Get Course Recommendations

Based on your profile, we recommend the best courses tailored to your needs. These courses are selected from a wide range of subjects, ensuring they match your level of knowledge and goals.

Step 4: Start Learning and Track Progress

Enroll in your recommended courses and start learning at your own pace. Our platform provides tools to track your progress, set goals, and achieve milestones along your educational journey.
